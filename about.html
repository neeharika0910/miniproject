About Us - MedicWaste

Our Mission

To reduce medicine waste and bridge the gap between surplus medicines and individuals who need them, ensuring equitable healthcare access for all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no usable medicine goes to waste, and every underprivileged individual has access to essential medications through an efficient donation and redistribution system.

The Problem

Every year, millions of medicines go unused and are discarded while many people struggle to afford essential medications. The lack of a structured donation system leads to significant waste and missed opportunities to improve healthcare access.

Our Team & Partners

MedicWaste is powered by a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, technologists, and volunteers who are committed to making a difference. We collaborate with NGOs, pharmacies, and medical institutions to ensure proper redistribution of donated medicines.
